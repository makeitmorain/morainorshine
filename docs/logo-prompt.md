# Morain or Shine -- Logo Design Prompt

## Brief

Design a minimal, modern wordmark logo for "morain or shine" -- a personal brand website covering baseball analytics, AI, coaching technology, and software development.

## Style

Clean, bold, modern sans-serif typography. Sports-tech energy without being cartoonish. Think tech startup meets athletic brand. No mascots, no illustrations, no baseball imagery in the main logo -- the name carries the brand. The wordmark should work as a single horizontal lockup and as a stacked two-line version.

## Color

Teal/cyan accent (#00BCD4 or similar) on a dark background (#0a0a0a). The wordmark should also work in white on dark and dark on white (mono versions).

## Personality

Confident, not corporate. Personality-forward. The name is a play on "rain or shine" + the last name Morain -- resilience, consistency, showing up.

## Format

Vector-clean edges. Must be legible at small sizes (favicon, mobile nav) and large sizes (hero banner, OG social card at 1200x630). Include a compact "MoS" or "M" monogram mark that works as a favicon and app icon.

## Do Not Include

Baseball imagery, gradients, 3D effects, drop shadows, script/cursive fonts, or generic tech iconography (circuits, gears, etc).

## Deliverables

- Horizontal wordmark (primary)
- Stacked two-line version
- Monogram mark (favicon/app icon)
- OG social card version (1200x630)
- Color versions: teal on dark, white on dark, dark on white

## Reference

Owner leans toward dark backgrounds, bold silhouettes, blue/teal/cyan color family. See existing sub-brand examples:
- RLM (Replacement Level Media): navy + gold
- xFP Lab (Medium): deep blue + green
- Pitcher Lyfe (retired): teal/cyan pitcher silhouette -- the "esports style" version was the favorite
