# Morain or Shine -- Logo Design Prompt (v2)

## Brief

Design a logo for "morain or shine" -- a personal brand for a baseball coach turned data scientist turned AI builder. The site covers baseball analytics, AI coaching tools, absurdist AI sports media, and software projects. The name is a play on "rain or shine" + the last name Morain. The brand ethos is: show up, grind, build things, keep moving forward.

## Personality

This is NOT a corporate brand or a polished tech startup. This is a try-hard who builds things out of obsession and grit. Think:
- The energy of a guy who built a pitching lab with a Wii Balance Board and PSEye cameras because there was no budget
- "Barstool meets Moneyball" crossed with a developer's terminal
- Confident but self-aware. Competitive but honest about the struggle.
- The tagline is "So what, next pitch." -- resilience, forward momentum, no dwelling

The logo should feel like it belongs on a hoodie you'd actually wear, not a business card you'd hand out at a conference.

## Style Direction

Bold, slightly raw, with personality. NOT minimal-corporate. NOT generic tech. Options to explore:

**Option A -- Typographic with attitude:** A wordmark with character. Could use a bold condensed or slightly distressed/textured typeface. Think athletic brand meets indie studio. The "or" in "morain or shine" could be treated differently (smaller, lighter, different color) to create visual rhythm.

**Option B -- Mark + wordmark:** A simple iconic mark alongside the text. The mark should be abstract/geometric -- NOT a literal baseball or pitcher silhouette. Could reference forward motion, grinding, weather (rain/shine duality), or a pitch in flight. Something that works at 32x32.

**Option C -- Monogram-forward:** A bold "MoS" or stylized "M" that carries the brand at small sizes, with the full wordmark as a secondary lockup. The monogram should have enough personality to stand alone -- not just Helvetica in a circle.

## Color

Primary: Teal/cyan (#00BCD4) -- this is locked in on the site.
Dark background: #0a0a0a
Must also work in:
- White on dark (primary use case -- dark-themed site)
- Dark on white (print, light contexts)
- Single color (merch, embroidery)

Open to a secondary accent if it serves the design (a warm tone to contrast the teal -- amber, orange, or gold could work and would tie to the RLM sub-brand).

## What the Logo Needs to Survive

- Favicon at 32x32 and 16x16 (the mark/monogram)
- Mobile nav at ~40px height
- Hero banner on the homepage
- OG social card at 1200x630
- Potential merch (hoodie, hat, sticker)
- Works on dark backgrounds primarily

## Do Not Include

- Literal baseball imagery (bats, balls, diamonds, stitching) -- the sub-brands handle that
- Script/cursive fonts -- doesn't match the voice
- Thin/light typefaces -- needs weight and presence
- Generic tech symbols (circuits, brackets, code syntax)
- Gradients or gloss effects
- Anything that looks like it was made in Canva in 5 minutes

## Reference Points (vibe, not direct copy)

- **The Ringer** -- bold, personality-forward media brand
- **Barstool Sports** -- unapologetic energy (but cleaner execution)
- **100 Thieves** -- esports/athletic crossover aesthetic (Zac's favorite direction from his old logos was the "esports style" Pitcher Lyfe mark)
- **Vercel / Linear** -- clean dark-mode tech aesthetic (for the site context, not the logo itself)

## Owner's Visual Taste

From existing logos Zac has designed or chosen:
- Gravitates toward dark backgrounds with bold silhouettes
- Blue/teal/cyan color family is home base
- Liked the "esports style" Pitcher Lyfe logo best -- dark navy with cyan pitcher, bold script, strong presence
- The xFP Lab logos used a batter silhouette in a circular frame -- clean but narrower than where the brand is now
- Wants something that feels more like a personal brand than a niche sports logo

## Deliverables

- Primary logo (wordmark + mark)
- Mark only (monogram/icon for favicon, app icon, small contexts)
- Horizontal lockup
- Stacked version
- OG social card layout (1200x630)
- Color versions: teal on dark, white on dark, dark on white, single color
